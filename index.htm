Hi-Lo

A vanilla JS game by Brady Vossler

Welcome to Hi-Lo!

This game is simple; you'll need to guess numbers between 0 and 1,000 until you correctly guess the answer.

  1. Press 'Start Game' to begin
  2. Enter your guess
  3. Use the feedback given to hone in your guess until you win!
